Zak Goldstein is a creatively motivated designer and illustrator from Dallas, TX. He studied studio art in Boston at the Tufts' SMFA program, embracing a multimedia approach to working and developing a deep appreciation for the graphic arts early on. Passionate about design, he interned at the Nasher Sculpture Center and Methodist Hospital in his home town to hone his skills. Here, and on his personal projects, Zak took pride in his attention to detail, witty creativity, and collaborative spirit. Outside of his professional practice, he creates elaborate pieces at the intersection of humor and aesthetics, finding funny and compositionally pleasing ways of presenting wordplay. Recently graduated, Zak is excited to bring his experience into the professional world as he looks to pursue a full-time arts career.
